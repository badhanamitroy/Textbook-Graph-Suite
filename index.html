About the Developer

Badhan Roy — developer of Textbook-Graph-Suite.

✨ Introducing Textbook-Graph-Suite — a tool built from the real challenges I faced while preparing lecture sheets and question papers during my work with several ed-tech organizations like 10 minute School, Victory Battles, ACS Future School.

Again and again, I needed graphs and coordinate figures that looked exactly like traditional textbook visuals. Tools like Desmos are great, but their style didn’t match the strict academic formatting required. I even tried solving this with Python earlier, but it still wasn’t efficient enough — and I often ended up drawing graphs manually, which was slow and impractical.

While learning JavaScript, I finally decided to create my own solution — every part was redesigned and refined to match authentic textbook styling.
